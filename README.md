# 🏛️ Operating-Analytics-Framework

> **A TOGAF-aligned System for Strategic Analytics, Quantitative Modeling, and Enterprise Architecture.**

This repository serves as a professional **Knowledge Management System (KMS)** and a **Strategic Toolbox**. It is designed to bridge the gap between high-level statistical research and executive-level business operations, providing a library of **Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs)** for data-driven decision making.

---

## 🎯 Vision & Methodology
The framework is built on the principle of the **T-Shaped Professional**: combining broad horizontal expertise in Business Strategy and Systems Architecture with deep vertical mastery in Quantitative Modeling.

Every asset within this repository follows a rigorous **End-to-End Validation Process**:
1. **Orientation** (Business Case Identification) 
2. **Decomposition** (Mental Mapping & Logical Layering)
3. **Implementation** (Production-ready Scripting)

---

## 🏗️ Framework Architecture
Following the **TOGAF** (The Open Group Architecture Framework) logic, the repository is organized into functional layers to ensure scalability and professional clarity.

### 📁 Business_Architecture
*Focus: Governance, Process Mapping, and Operating Systems.*
- Enterprise-level workflows and SOP documentation.
- Business Process Modeling (BPMN) and Strategic Frameworks.
- Integration of Notion-based Management Systems.

### 📁 Models_for_Business
*Focus: Tactical execution and Value Creation.*
- **Marketing Analytics:** Bass Diffusion Models, Customer Segmentation (Clustering).
- **Operations:** Supply Chain optimization and Churn Analysis.
- **Strategic ROI:** Data-driven decision frameworks for growth.

### 📁 Time_Series_and_Forecasting
*Focus: Market Equilibrium and Volatility Dynamics.*
- **Econometrics:** Cointegration, VECM, and Transfer Function Models (TFM).
- **Risk Management:** Advanced GARCH modeling (including manual Gradient/Hessian optimization).
- **Duration Analysis:** Accelerated Failure Time (AFT) models.

### 📁 Inference_and_Regression
*Focus: Causal Discovery and Probabilistic Reasoning.*
- **Statistical Foundations:** Linear/Generalized Linear Models (GLM) and Hierarchical modeling.
- **Complex Distributions:** Zero-Inflated and Negative Binomial models for count data.
- **Non-Linearity:** Semi-parametric modeling using Splines for flexible inference.

### 📁 Machine_Learning_and_NLP
*Focus: Pattern Recognition at Scale.*
- **Deep Learning:** Transformers (BERT) and Neural Network architectures.
- **Supervised Learning:** Gradient Boosting, Random Forests, and Decision Trees.
- **Unstructured Data:** Natural Language Processing for clinical and business text classification.

### 📁 Data_Engineering_and_Cleaning
*Focus: Data Integrity and Automation.*
- Automated Pre-processing pipelines and Naming Conventions.
- Outlier detection and missing data imputation strategies.

---

## 🛠️ Operational Standards
To ensure "Plug-and-Play" usability in a corporate consulting environment, all scripts adhere to the following standards:
- **Naming Convention:** Systematic file naming (e.g., `01_model_name_PS.R`) for version control.
- **Clean Code:** Production-ready scripts stripped of academic noise, focusing on input-process-output.
- **Strategic Tips:** Inline documentation focusing on model stability, convergence, and business interpretation.
